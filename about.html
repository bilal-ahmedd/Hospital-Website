About Us

Medical specialty concerned with the care of acutely ill hospitalized patients

The Cambridge Hospital (TCH) began in 1848 as Victoria’s first public hospital. And while we only had 10 beds to our name, we had the community of many behind us, and we were ready to provide the best possible care for those in need.
